About


I am an economist currently working at the United Nations Development Programme on multidimensional poverty, inequality, and human development metrics. I have a B.A. in Economics and an MSc in Global Health and Economics from Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health .

My research focuses on addressing various aspects of development, including human capital development, poverty reduction, and social protection. I am also interested in designing mathematical and computational decision-making models to design effective social policies that create positive and sustainable impacts on individuals, communities, and societies.
